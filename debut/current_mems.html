Our Members

"IEEE-Eta Kappa Nu is recognized throughout the profession and Eta Kappa Nu members carry with them the distinction of superior scholarship, attitude, and character. Employers seek out IEEE-Eta Kappa Nu chapters and graduates and generally have a close recruiting relationship with chapters. Chapters often publish “Resume Books” and organize focused recruitment fairs, tech talks, and tours just to get to know the IEEE-Eta Kappa Nu students. It is our tradition to help each other, to network, to find work, to advise, and to guide. Alumni chapters in particular were started to help fellow members in their professional lives." - HKN Website
